TOKENIZATION

You can create unique non-fungible tokens (NFT) for your aircraft or yacht to immutably store all info on the asset's specification, ownership rights and sales transaction on blockchain.
Use this blockchain instrument to successfuly initiate, negotiate and close sales deals on the platform under the control of smart contracts providing privacy but transparency, efficiency but safety.
To get full info on the plane and the owner, a buyer must reserve NFT and enter into negotiations.
After the deal is closed your NFT will contain all documents on the transaction.
