HOW IT WORKS

Our all-in-one solution allows you to sell or buy your aircraft, yacht or car on your own online without assistance of third-parties. All processes are simple but comrehensive.

GLOSSARY OF KEY TERMS

Make deposits, payments and withdrawals in cryptocurrencies right from your Jeto Vault. Get access to a history of payments. Take full control over your assets. Integration with banks for fiat payments is coming.

What is NFT?

Non-fungible token contains the following data and documents: Aircraft specification Brochure (if available) Public offer signed by the owner Proof-of-ownership docs As a result of the transaction the following docs will be added to NFT's metadata: Aircraft purchase agreement Inspection report (if available) Bill of sale
